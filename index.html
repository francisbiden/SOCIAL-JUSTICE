Fake Promises

Leaders who make false promises to secure votes betray the trust of the people. Such deceit undermines democracy and stalls progress, leaving communities disillusioned and neglected. Genuine leadership should be built on integrity, not manipulation. Empty promises only deepen the cycle of corruption and failure.

Ethics and anti-corruption Commission of Kenya (EACC)

The Ethics and Anti-Corruption Commission of Kenya is failing in its mandate to combat corruption. Weak enforcement and lack of accountability allow corrupt practices to persist, eroding public trust and hindering meaningful progress in governance.

Ruthless kenya Police

The brutality of the Kenya Police has become a serious concern, with numerous cases of excessive force, harassment, and extra-judicial killings reported. Such actions undermine public trust in law enforcement and violate human rights. Instead of protecting citizens, police brutality often perpetuates fear, especially during protests and security operations. There is a pressing need for police reforms, accountability, and stronger oversight to curb these abuses and restore the integrity of the police force, ensuring that they serve the public justly and professionally.

Free Media

In many parts of Africa, the media and justice system face increasing threats from authoritarian regimes, corrupt officials, and political interference. Journalists are frequently harassed, censored, or detained for exposing corruption or criticizing the government. This suppression stifles freedom of expression and denies the public access to vital information. Similarly, the justice system is often compromised through political manipulation, corruption, and intimidation, preventing fair trials and impartial rulings. Such attacks on these essential pillars of democracy erode accountability, weaken governance, and harm the fight for justice and human rights across the continent.

Human Life at stake

Human life is gravely at stake when extra-judicial killings and excessive force by police occur. These unlawful actions violate fundamental rights and erode the trust between law enforcement and citizens. Victims, often targeted without due process, face lethal consequences, while families and communities live in fear. Instead of protecting society, these brutal practices foster a culture of impunity and injustice, leading to widespread human rights abuses. Such actions demand urgent reforms, accountability, and justice to protect lives and uphold the rule of law.
